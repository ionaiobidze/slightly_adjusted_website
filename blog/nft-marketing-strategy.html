NFT

NFTs have gained widespread acceptance and have become an important part of the web3 economy. Non-fungible tokens are stored on a blockchain protocol like Ethereum, Solana, Cardano, etc., and use smart contracts to prove ownership of virtual or physical assets like photos, videos, tweets, music, and even memes.

Working with an NFT marketing agency can significantly enhance the success of your NFT launch by providing expertise, resources, and a comprehensive strategy.

NFTs enable the commercialization of digital assets such as art pieces, music, files, online games, and so on. NFTs are available to everyone; anything can be sold as an NFT, and anyone can create one. If you are an artist, content creator, or brand, you can use NFTs to sell your works directly to your audience, eliminating the need for intermediaries.

According to NiftyTable, OpenSea has 6.8 million Ethereum and Polygon-based NFT collections, but only 15K have ever been sold, accounting for 0.2% of the total number of NFTs. This number is likely to go up when we consider the number of NFT collections on other blockchain protocols such as Solana, WAX, Cardano, etc. People like to talk about how 99% of NFTs will go to zero, but 99.8% of NFTs have no market value already. The sheer number of NFT collections being launched has overwhelmed the NFT market. Because of this, there is a need for NFT marketing strategies that can get NFTs in front of the right people.

7. Submit your NFT collection on drop listing websites

NFT calendars are websites that NFT collectors use to research and find out about future NFT releases on various NFT marketplaces. To reach potential collectors and communities, make sure all of your drops are listed on NFT calendars. While there are numerous NFT calendar websites, you can begin with this list of the best NFT calendar websites for your NFT launch.

NFT calendars consolidate all of the best NFT drops from various NFT marketplaces into one location, eliminating the need for NFT collectors to search multiple marketplaces for the latest and greatest NFT releases. Filling out a simple form with information about your release is all that is required to be added to the NFT calendar. Listing your NFT collection on NFT calendars is completely free and can provide you with a lot of exposure.

8. Email marketing

E-mail marketing is an excellent marketing strategy to communicate with your target audience about your NFT collection. You can send personalized email newsletter campaigns to your newsletter subscribers on a weekly or monthly basis to keep them up-to-date on events and insights related to your NFT collection. With email marketing, your goal should be to tell people about new products or highlights while also asking for feedback or getting them to join in.

Newsletters are distributed by the majority of NFT marketplaces, calendars, and blockchain-related media outlets. Some even accept requests to include new NFT releases. Most NFT platforms will offer paid options for this type of advertisement, but some media outlets, such as NFT Calendar, will feature NFT drops for free. This is an excellent marketing opportunity because you get to market yourself in front of users who are both a highly targeted audience and interested in NFTs.

9. Create a whitelisting strategy

Whitelisting is an NFT marketing strategy that allows a select group of community members early access to your NFT drop at a lower price than the general public. Whitelist campaigns are a great way to see how well received your product is likely to be by the NFT community at large and use that feedback to determine whether or not to expand your marketing efforts.
