What is a press release?

A press release is an official statement issued by a business to news media outlets and independent publishers to provide information about a specific subject, make an official statement, or make an announcement to a specific audience.

A press release published on a top crypto-centric news website is an excellent way to quickly draw attention to your brand. Because press releases are typically written by the company or a marketing agency on their behalf, they provide a lot of control over the message in the content.

A press release is an important communication and marketing method that can be used to strengthen relationships with news and media outlets, generate social proof, and increase SEO traffic.

When done correctly, a press release can be a powerful tool for spreading the word about the exciting things your business is doing such as launching a new NFT, token, cryptocurrency, or blockchain platform. Utilizing crypto press release distribution ensures your message reaches a wide array of crypto news sites, international news outlets, and authority online news media sites.

Types of press release for crypto projects

1. New product or token launch

This type of press release is useful for spreading the word about new solutions, such as a DApp or NFT, that your web3 project will soon be offering. It is critical to include key information in the product/token announcement press release, such as:

  • Specifications
  • Launch date
  • Pricing
  • Tokens available
  • Utility
  • Any other details that your target audience should know about

2. Updates and announcements

Product updates and expansions, like new product launches, are prime candidates for promotion. Explain the change, why it was implemented, and how it benefits the consumer.

3. Crypto events

Event press releases provide specific details about an event within the crypto space so that their target audience can be informed. These types of press releases are an important part of event marketing because they attract coverage from news sources and other media outlets.

Key details to include:

  • What the event is about
  • Location
  • Timing
  • Who should consider attending
  • Pricing, etc

4. New partnerships

Putting out a press release announcing a collaboration is a win-win promotion strategy for both parties involved. To execute this type of release effectively, write an overview about both brands, why the partnership was formed, who benefits, and any other key information for future and current stakeholders.

Take, as an example, the press release published by the major cryptocurrency exchange Binance announcing their collaboration with Cristiano Ronaldo in releasing his first NFT collection.

5. Executive promotion and hiring

Executives typically serve as the company's public face, and a press release helps to kick off their new role. This form of news release is designed to establish an individual's or an institution's credibility in a given domain.

It may include biographical information, a photo, and other essential elements to show their reputation.

6. Awards

Press releases announcing accolades and successes help to establish your organization as an authority in your field.

A news release of this type includes information about the firm and why they were given the award, as well as facts about the award itself and the event.

2. Find your hook

When writing a press release, the most crucial thing to do first is to choose a hook that will entice your readers and journalists to open¬†your crypto press release. Working on a hook is essential if you want your press release to be noteworthy, and it also raises the likelihood of your press release being shareable on social media.

3. Catchy headline

Because the headline of your press release is the first thing people will read, having a catchy headline is essential for success. You just have one line to work with, which can be intimidating, but think about your words carefully to make your headline interesting.

Most importantly, make it interesting: Remember that journalists receive dozens, if not hundreds, of press releases each day, so take the time to develop a fascinating headline. The particular headline you select is very dependent on what you're announcing. Try to keep it brief. This makes it easier to read and more visually appealing.

Here's an example of a headline we created for Wolves of Rome to announce the launch of their trading card NFT collection:

4. Add context with your sub-headline

Most press release distribution websites allow you to include a subheadline with your release. However, sub-headlines are frequently overlooked by writers, but we strongly advise taking advantage of this important space. Basically, a sub-headline contextualizes your headline.

A solid sub-headline should answer the most irate readers' questions about why you're publishing the press release. It should be long enough to cover the important while remaining concise.

5. It is time to write the body copy

The body of your press release should begin with a lead. A lead is the first paragraph of your press release and can make or break a news release in terms of media coverage. The lead should begin with the most interesting content in your release. Consider your lead to be the elevator pitch for what your brand is doing new. Make it as appealing as possible.

Use the opening paragraph to introduce:

  • Purpose of the announcement
  • An important stat or figure in your industry
  • A new finding in your industry
  • New cryptocurrency or blockchain technology

After creating an engaging introduction, the next step is to dig into additional specifics about your press release. Give your readers the vital insights and background information they need to understand why this press release is newsworthy.

Details that improve your narrative, such as creative or notable ways your organization produced the project or news at hand¬†should be included here. Alternatively, if applicable, comment on the long-term effects of your announcement.

Newsjacking is another effective approach to add value to your press release. It is a¬†method of making your press release more valuable to the writer and reader by linking it to something that is trending.

They add a different perspective to the text, making it more diverse, and they can quickly make your work more engaging and readable.

2. Cite sources

Make sure to cite any external sources you include in your press release. This will increase your credibility in the eyes of your viewers. It is critical to ensure that everything you are linking to is legitimate.

Unless you‚Äôre citing your sources at the bottom of your press releases, we don‚Äôt advocate displaying the entire URL. Make your links appear more natural by using anchor text.

3. Include data and stats (journalists love it!)

Data and statistics are excellent tools to gain attention since journalists can quickly include them in their stories. Data and statistics offer something new to the table that is supported by real-world data.

Furthermore, unlike most dull press releases, the data and statistics should be about something intriguing that journalists will be interested in covering. If you conducted your own research, you should incorporate it because it demonstrates that your company is knowledgeable about the subject or industry.

4. Use bullet points

You can utilize bullet points at the beginning of your press release to describe the key points. Keep bullet points to 1-3 medium-length crisp phrases, with keywords and supporting keywords included.

6. Add multimedia

Visuals may be a great way to grab people's attention, so don't be hesitant to incorporate them in your press release. Add pictures to the story to help it stand out. When you do, make certain that the graphics you select are high-quality, high-resolution¬†images. Visuals in a press release are a wonderful PR strategy, but they must be relevant and aesthetically appealing to achieve the desired outcomes.

7. Boilerplate

The boilerplate is your company's short bio, and it is the best strategy to end your press release. It should appear on every press release you issue. In the first paragraph, describe what your brand performs in simple terms and offer a link to your company's website.

Ideally, you should have the following details in your boilerplate:

  • Your brand name
  • A mission statement
  • Founding dates
  • An update on the mission statement

Every press release requires contact information. Bloggers and journalists who want to write about your news can then contact you for more information or a quote.

Make sure to include the following details:

  • Point of contact: Name and job title of the person to contact for additional information
  • Email address: Give them the best option for contacting the selected point of contact. You could also mention your email¬†address.
  • Phone number: In case they'd prefer the phone to email.

Important tips when writing a crypto press release

  • Always write in third person (Basically don't use ‚ÄúI‚Äù, ‚Äúwe‚Äù, or ‚Äúyou‚Äù).
  • Your press release should be written in the style of a newspaper article. For your press release, you can utilize the AP style format, which is commonly used by journalists.
  • Make sure your release is formatted for skimmers.
  • Because you can't easily go back and make changes to a release after it's live, make sure it's properly edited and error-free.
  • Find journalists and writers who wish to write about your project and share your press release with them directly if you want more media outlets to cover it.
  • Keep it short and to the point, ideally, no longer than 500 words.
